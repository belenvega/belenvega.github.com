Healthy banana waffles!

10 min

Ingrediants

  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • pinch of baking powder

Instructions

  1. Smash the banana until it's turned into a pudding consistency
  2. Mix the smashed banana, two eggs, and pinch of baking powder in a large bowl until everything is evenly blended. Make sure the mixture is thick enough to hold when placed in to the waffle maker.
  3. Pour enough of the waffle mix into the waffle maker and leave until it's cooked.
